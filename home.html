About

XPO Logistics Limited is a prominent international logistics company and solutions provider, covering freight forwarding, transportation, and customs clearance.

XPO Logistics, which has a significant national footprint, has been providing services to various industries such as automotive, engineering, yarns, textiles, pharmaceutical, commodities, and fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) for over 22 years.

XPO Logistics collaborates with a wide range of partners to provide dependable, effective, and timely cargo movement around the globe using an asset-light business model.

In 2000, XPO Logistics was established with the intention of serving as a one-stop shop for international logistics and in response to the need for a dependable, morally-driven player in the Indian logistics scene. XPO operates as a cost-innovation and personalized service provider.
